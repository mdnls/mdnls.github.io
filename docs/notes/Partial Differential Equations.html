Classifications of PDEs

For some PDEs, the structure of the differential operator forces the solution to be more smooth than the boundary conditions. These classes are

  1. Elliptic: ex. Laplace & Poisson equation, Helmholtz Equation
  2. Parabolic: ex. Heat equation
  3. Hyperbolic: ex. Wave equation

Physical Phenomenon

Some physical phenomenon correspond to specific PDE forms:

  • Advection: $\partial_t u = \nabla u$
  • Diffusion: $\partial_t u = \Delta u$
  • Waves: $\partial_{tt} u = \Delta u$.
