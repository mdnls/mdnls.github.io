Transportation Inequalities

Description: this page is a collection of notes related to transportation inequalities.

Pinsker's Inequality: can be viewed as a T1 inequality under the metric $d(x, y) = 1 - \mathbb{1}[x=y]$, since this metric induces the Wasserstein distance $W_d(P, Q) = \|P-Q\|_{\mathsf{TV}}$. $$ \|P - Q\|_{\mathsf{TV}} \leq \sqrt{2 \KL(P \mid Q) } $$ Bounds via Donsker-Varadhan: we know that for bounded $f$ it holds $$ \KL(P \mid Q) + \log \E_Q[\exp(f)] \geq \E_P[f] $$ really, you can think of this as super-duality. P, f should be dual to each other, and now we think about what happens when we evaluate them on functionals related to Q

Observe that $\lim \E_Q [\exp(\eps f)] - \eps \E_P[f] \to \E_Q[f] - \Q_[F]$ as $\eps \to 0$.
