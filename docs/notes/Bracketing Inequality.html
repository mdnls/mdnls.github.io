Bracketing Inequality

Let $\Fcl$ be a model class, let $X_1, \ldots, X_n \sim \mu$ i.i.d., and consider the empirical process $(G_n(f))_{f \in \Fcl}$ defined by $$ G_n(f) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\sum_{i=1}^n (f(X_i) - \E_\mu[f(X)]) $$ Bracketing techniques can be used to control the suprema $\E[\sup_{f \in \Fcl}G_n(f)]$ in a way that might improve on techniques like Dudley's Entropy Integral. For more discussion, see Chaining Inequalities.

Definition (bracket). We say that $\Rcl$ is an $\eps$-bracket of $\Fcl$ in the norm $\|\cdot \|$ if the following hold:

  1. The bracket is a sequence of pairs: $\Rcl = \{(f^+_i, f^-_i) \subset \Fcl : i = 1 \ldots |\Rcl|\}$.
  2. Each $f \in \Fcl$ is sandwiched by some bracket: there exists $i \in [|\Rcl|]$ so $f_i^-(x) \leq f(x) \leq f^+_i(x)$ for all $x \in \Xcl$.
  3. The brackets are close in $\| \cdot\|$: for all $i = 1 \ldots |\Rcl|$, we have $\|f_i^+ - f_i^-\| \leq \eps$.

We define the bracketing number $\Ncl_{[]}(\Fcl, \| \cdot \|, \eps)$ as the minimum cardinality of any $\eps$-bracket of $\Fcl$.

The bracketing number $\Ncl_{[]}$ plays a role similar to that of the covering number in a chaining inequality. In a chaining bound, the strategy is to approximate $G_n(f)$ by a process $G_n(\pi(f))$, where $\pi(f)$ projects $f$ onto an $\eps$-cover of $\Fcl$. The error term $G_n(f) - G_n(\pi(f))$ is controlled by a property like lipschitzness, and the supremum over $\pi(\Fcl)$ is controlled by chaining with a Discrete Maximal Inequality. The key difference of the bracketing bound is that the remainder term is controlled in a different way.

Proposition (one-step bracketing inequality). We have the following bracket-based analogue of the One-step Chaining Inequality: $$ \E\left[\sup_{f \in \Fcl} G_n(f)\right]\leq \inf_{\eps \geq 0} \left\{ \eps \sqrt{n} + \sup_{f \in \Fcl} \|f\|_\infty \sqrt{2 \, \Ncl_{[]}(\Fcl, \| \cdot \|_{\Lcl^1(\mu)}, \eps)}\right\} $$

Proof. Fix $\eps > 0$ and let $\Rcl$ be a minimal $\eps$-bracket in $\Lcl^1(\mu)$ of $\Fcl$. Let $\pi : \Fcl \to [|\Rcl|]$ return, for each $f \in \Fcl$, the index $\pi(f) \in [|\Rcl|]$ such that $f_{\pi(f)}^- \preccurlyeq f \preccurlyeq f_{\pi(f)}^+$. Then we have, $$ \begin{aligned} G_n(f) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\sum_{i=1}^n f(X_i) - \mu(f) & \leq \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\sum_{i=1}^n (f^+_{\pi(f)}(X_i) - \E_\mu[f^-_{\pi(f)}]) \\ & \leq \sqrt{n}\, \E_\mu[f_{\pi(f)}^+ (X) - f_{\pi(f)}^-(X)] + G_n(f^+) \end{aligned} $$
